// About

Research-driven trading, transparently tracked.

What we do

BelFed Analytics publishes research-driven trade ideas across global stocks and crypto, with weekly market commentary and full performance tracking. Every trade is recorded — wins and losses — in R-multiples (risk-adjusted units) so the track record reflects how a disciplined trader would actually compound capital.

How we work

We focus on high-conviction setups: clear thesis, defined entry and stop, and a price target. Position sizing is normalized by risk, not dollar size. We publish what worked and what didn't, and explain why.

Who it's for

Self-directed investors and traders who want institutional-grade idea flow without the noise of "guru" Twitter — and who value transparent process over big claims.
