Performance methodology

Performance is reported in R-multiples — risk-adjusted units where 1R equals the dollar amount risked from entry to stop on a given trade. A trade that hits its stop is recorded as −1R; a trade that hits target at 3x risk is recorded as +3R.

  • Returns shown (e.g. +85.96R) are cumulative R across all closed trades for the period.
  • Win rate is closed-trade-based: trades closed at a positive R are wins.
  • Open positions are not included in the cumulative R until they close.
  • R-based reporting is independent of position size — actual dollar returns will vary based on the risk-per-trade you choose.
